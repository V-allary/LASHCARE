How to Care for Your Lash Extensions

Beauty Tips • Lashcare • Aftercare

Lash extensions are an investment in your beauty, and proper care ensures they last longer while keeping your natural lashes healthy.

1. Avoid Water for the First 24 Hours

After your lash appointment, avoid water, steam, or sweat for at least 24 hours. This allows the adhesive to fully cure.

2. Be Gentle with Your Lashes

Avoid rubbing your eyes or pulling at your lashes. Always cleanse gently using lash-safe products.

3. Skip Oil-Based Products

Oil-based makeup removers can weaken lash adhesive, causing premature shedding.

4. Book Regular Refills

To maintain a full look, schedule refills every 2–3 weeks with your lash technician.

With proper aftercare, your lash extensions will remain beautiful, lightweight, and long-lasting.
